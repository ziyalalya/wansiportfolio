About BizMarket

This platform is a campus-based marketplace that connects students, teachers, and vendors in one system. It allows easy buying and selling of products within the school environment.

🎯 Our Goal

To provide a simple, organized, and efficient way for campus users to buy and sell products without hassle.

✨ Features

  • Product marketplace with search and category filter
  • Vendor product posting and management
  • Cart and checkout system
  • Order tracking for students and teachers
  • Vendor order approval system
  • Notifications and chat system

👨‍💻 Developers

This system was developed as a student project to demonstrate a complete marketplace workflow using web technologies.

Help & Support

If you're experiencing issues, check the solutions below:

🔑 Account Issues

Make sure your username, password, and selected role are correct.

🛍️ Ordering Problems

  • Ensure the product is approved by the vendor
  • Check if the item has available stock
  • Try refreshing the page

📦 Vendor Issues

  • Confirm your product after adding it
  • Check the Approved list
  • Make sure all fields are filled correctly

🔔 Notifications

Click the bell icon to see updates about your orders.

💬 Chat Support

Use the chat feature to communicate with other users.

📧 Contact

For more help, contact your system administrator.
